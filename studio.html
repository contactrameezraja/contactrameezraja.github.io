Not every insight belongs in a dashboard.
Some patterns deserve to be felt, not just filtered.

Algorithmic Sketches is where I explore the space between machine learning and visual storytelling, turning model outputs, streaming data, and complex signals into visual pieces that make you pause. Think of it as the creative lab behind the engineering: experiments in D3.js, generative visuals, and data-driven art that reveal the hidden shapes in the numbers.

What drives me is a belief that data can do more than inform. It can move people. I want to bridge the gap between cold statistics and human connection, turning complex information into experiences that awaken empathy and inspire action. Not just accessible, but memorable. Not just accurate, but meaningful.
